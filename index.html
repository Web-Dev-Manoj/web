Person Identification using Face and Speech Recognition for Visually impaired Persons
My Contribution - Checkout here

The proposed system and methodology aim to assist individuals with visual impairments in identifying persons and objects in their vicinity. This method involves several steps, beginning with the reception of auditory signals emitted by individuals present in the vicinity of the visually impaired individual. Subsequently, the system proceeds to capture images of the individuals and objects within the surrounding environment.
